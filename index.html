Who we are &

Why choose us

Mandeep's Architecture studio is a Montreal based, full-service architectural firm specializing in residential, commercial, institutional and industrial projects. We believe professional knowledge, collaboration and utmost level of excellence in service are essential in creating long-term relationship with our clients and consultants.

We offer design, research, development, technical support and construction administration for buildings in Montreal & all around globe. We manage all phases of our projects, from design to construction, and we are always happy to work within our client's budgets and timelines.

We pride ourselves on the unique and customized process tailored for the needs of every client. We work closely with our clients through every stage of the project to ensure exceptional quality, efficient design and effective cost management including the use of cutting edge technology to provide our clients with unprecedented customer service and design experience.
